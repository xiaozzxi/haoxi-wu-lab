Join Us

We're always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our team.

Postdoctoral Positions

We welcome applications from postdoctoral researchers with backgrounds in biochemistry, structural biology, cell biology, or related fields. Experience with membrane proteins is beneficial but not required.

Funding opportunities include EMBO, Marie Curie, and Newton fellowships.

Graduate Students

Prospective DPhil students should apply through the University of Oxford graduate admissions. We accept students through the Biochemistry and interdisciplinary doctoral training programmes. We also welcome rotation and Masters students interested in research projects.

See Biochemistry Graduate Admissions for details.
