Haoxi grew up in China and attended Sichuan University for his bachelor's degree. During university, he was an exchange student at the National University of Singapore. Haoxi completed his PhD with Gia Voeltz at the University of Colorado Boulder and did his postdoctoral training with Manu Hegde at the Medical Research Council Laboratory of Molecular Biology.
